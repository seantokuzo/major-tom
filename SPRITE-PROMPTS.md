Copy-paste these into ChatGPT (DALL-E) to generate sprites.

---

## Style Guide

All sprites must follow these rules for consistency:
- **Style**: FTL / Stardew Valley pixel art
- **Background**: TRANSPARENT (no gray, no gradient, no glow)
- **Layout**: 2x2 grid — front (top-left), back (top-right), left (bottom-left), right (bottom-right)
- **Edges**: Crisp hard pixel edges, NO anti-aliasing, NO blur, NO soft shadows
- **Size**: Characters at ~32x32 pixels within each cell. Furniture/props at 64x64 per cell.
- **Perspective**: Slightly top-down ¾ view (same as characters)
